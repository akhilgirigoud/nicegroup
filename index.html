Who we are

Fantastic food

Nice Fish and Feeds is India's foremost producer of poultry bone meal, poultry oil, and biograde oil, serving a wide range of industries with unmatched quality and consistency.
Our journey began with a vision to utilize poultry by-products sustainably and create high-value feed and industrial solutions. Today, we are trusted by businesses across India and globally for our cutting-edge manufacturing processes, robust supply chain, and commitment to environmental stewardship.
